# 🚀 AI SDR Lead Generation & Outreach Automation

An end-to-end AI-powered SDR (Sales Development Representative) automation workflow built with **n8n**, **GPT-5.5**, **Tavily AI**, **Google Sheets**, and **Gmail API**.

This workflow automates the complete outbound sales process—from company research to generating personalized cold email drafts—helping businesses find qualified prospects faster and scale outreach efficiently.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🔍 AI-Powered Company Research
* 🏢 Company Intelligence Extraction
* 🎯 ICP Qualification (100-Point Scoring)
* 👤 AI Decision-Maker Identification
* ✅ Lead Enrichment & Validation
* 📈 Personalized Outreach Strategy
* ✉️ AI Cold Email Generation
* 📝 AI Email Quality Scoring & Regeneration
* 📧 Automatic Gmail Draft Creation
* 📊 Google Sheets Integration & Status Updates
